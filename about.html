About Carrington Physio

Your partner in health and wellness

Our Story

Founded with a passion for helping people move better and live healthier lives, Carrington Physio has been serving our community for over a decade. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality physiotherapy care, delivered with compassion and expertise.

Our clinic was established on the principle that healing is a partnership between therapist and patient. We take the time to understand your unique needs, goals, and challenges, creating personalized treatment plans that deliver real results.

Today, we're proud to be a trusted healthcare provider in the community, known for our evidence-based approach, commitment to excellence, and genuine care for every patient who walks through our doors.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional physiotherapy services that empower individuals to achieve optimal physical health and wellbeing. We are committed to delivering personalized, evidence-based care in a supportive and professional environment.

Our Vision

To be the leading physiotherapy clinic in our community, recognized for our clinical excellence, innovative treatment approaches, and unwavering commitment to patient-centered care that transforms lives.
